Entertainment

While most visitors come to Taniti for its stunning beaches, lush rainforests, and breathtaking volcano, the island offers a variety of entertainment options for those looking to explore beyond nature. Whether you’re into history, adventure, nightlife, or family-friendly activities, there’s something for everyone to enjoy.

Cultural & Educational Experiences

Visit our local museum and art galleries to learn about Taniti’s rich heritage.

Outdoor Adventures & Excursions

Go snorkeling, zip-lining, take a helicopter ride, or take a volcano tour to experience Taniti’s wild side.

Nightlife & Social Spots

Enjoy craft beer at a local microbrewery, relax at one of Taniti’s pubs, or Dance the night away at a new dance club featuring live DJs.

Family Fun & Recreational Activities

Visit the arcade, movie theater, or bowling alley for laid-back fun. Be on the look out for a nine-hole golf course coming soon!

Merriton Landing: The Hub of Entertainment

Many of these attractions are located in Merriton Landing, a rapidly developing area on the north side of Yellow Leaf Bay. This vibrant district is becoming the go-to spot for entertainment, dining, and nightlife on the island.

Whether you’re looking for thrilling outdoor adventures, a fun night out, or relaxing activities, Taniti has entertainment options to match every traveler’s interests.

Grocery Stores

Whether you’re stocking up for a long stay or just need a quick snack, Taniti offers several options for grocery shopping. From full-sized supermarkets to smaller convenience stores, visitors and locals alike can easily find fresh produce, pantry essentials, and snacks throughout the island.

Grocery Shopping Options in Taniti

Supermarkets – Taniti has two full-sized supermarkets that offer a wide variety of groceries, fresh produce, and household essentials. These are the best places to find everything you need in one stop.

Grocery Stores – For quick trips or local specialty items, two smaller grocery stores provide a more compact selection of essentials, often with a focus on fresh, locally sourced products.

24-Hour Convenience Store – If you need something late at night or early in the morning, Taniti has one convenience store open 24/7, offering snacks, drinks, and basic groceries for travelers on the go.

No matter where you are on the island, you’ll have access to the essentials you need for your stay. Whether you’re looking for fresh local ingredients, quick snacks, or household goods, Taniti’s grocery stores have you covered.

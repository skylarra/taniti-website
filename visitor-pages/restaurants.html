Restaurants

Taniti offers a diverse dining experience, blending local island flavors with international favorites. With 10 restaurants across the island, visitors can enjoy everything from freshly caught seafood to American comfort food and Pan-Asian cuisine.

Fresh Seafood & Rice

Taniti’s five seafood-focused restaurants serve up the best of the island’s local flavors, featuring freshly caught fish, traditional rice dishes, and tropical ingredients. These restaurants offer an authentic taste of Tanitian cuisine, perfect for those looking to experience island life through food.

American-Style Dining

For visitors craving familiar flavors, three restaurants on the island specialize in American-style meals. Whether you’re in the mood for a hearty breakfast, a classic burger, or a comforting plate of fries, these casual dining spots offer a taste of home.

Pan-Asian Cuisine

Bringing together bold flavors from across Asia, two restaurants in Taniti offer Pan-Asian dishes. These spots are perfect for those looking to explore rich, savory, and spicy flavors with a tropical twist.

Whether you’re in the mood for fresh seafood by the water, a classic American meal, or vibrant Asian flavors, Taniti’s growing food scene offers something for every traveler.
